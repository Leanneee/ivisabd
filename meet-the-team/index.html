Teresa Doherty

Teresa Doherty, Director

Teresa founded Five Star in November 2003 and over the next 14 years has overseen it's growth to become a leading Immigration Services provider. Her business experience is vast, and prior to setting up Five Star she worked for many years as Finance Director for a large Scottish Company. She brought these talents to Five Star, backed by strong Administrative skills and an instinctive grasp of what Business needs to succeed. Staff are encouraged to grow and develop their abilities to ensure the ethos of the Company always comes first – quite simply, the Client and their needs.

Dan Doherty

Dan Doherty, Director

Her brother, Dan, joined Five Star in early 2004 and has gained a wide range of knowledge and experience in all aspects of the business over the last 14 years. He has a keen eye for detail and is often called upon for the more complicated cases. He has a strong focus on ensuring that our Clients’ needs are looked after to the best of our ability and feels that the bond of trust between Five Star and our Clients is key to both the success of the Business and more importantly the happiness of the Client. He is an advocate of doing business in an open and honest manner and believes that the best outcome is always achieved by regular communication between both parties.

Shereen Shafaatulla

Shereen Shafaatulla, Senior In - House Solicitor

Shereen is our Senior In House Solicitor and specialises in Immigration and Asylum cases. She qualified in November 2006 and is able to provide immigration advice above and beyond Level 3 OISC. She is an experienced Court solicitor who has won cases in both the First Tier and Upper Tribunals. She joined us from the Immigration Advisory Service (IAS) and has been a member of the Five Star team since 2012. She is a member of the Law Society Sub Committee of Immigration and Asylum, a member of ILPA and a past Treasurer of the Scottish Legal Practicioners Association (SILPA). She directs all Legal aspects of the Business and particularly enjoys meeting with and providing the best advice for each Client, based on their individual or family circumstances. Shereen takes great personal pride and interest in achieving the best outcome for our clients and has a consistently high success rate in particularly challenging cases. She speaks Punjabi, Urdu and Hindi.

Pavan Sumal

Pavan Sumal, In - House Solicitor

Pavan graduated in 2009 and has since worked with the Ethnic Minorities Law Centre in Glasgow firstly as a Volunteer and then as a Trainee Solicitor before qualifying in 2013 and joining Five Star in 2014. Throughout her training and since qualifying Pavan has further developed her interest in Immigration Law and now specialises in this field as well as having experience in Employment Law. She has an analytical approach and an aptitude for researching and problem solving, both important aspects of the work she carries out in Five Star. Pavan is also a member of the Law Society Sub Committee of Immigration and Asylum and a member of ILPA. She speaks Punjabi, Urdu and Hindi.
